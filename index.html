Agent-based simulation of Alzheimer's disease spread

Alzheimer's disease is one of the most important mental illnesses. It is posited that more than 25% of the population is affected by mental disease during their lifetime. Each patient draws resources from the economy concerned. Therefore, it is important to quantify the potential economic threat.
